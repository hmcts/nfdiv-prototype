Do you want to claim divorce costs from your husband or wife?

You can ask the court to consider whether your husband or wife should pay some or all of the costs related to your divorce. These costs could include the application fee, solicitors fees, or any additional court fees.

You do not have to provide details of the costs now.

This is only asking you about costs relating to the divorce. How you divide money and property is dealt with separately.

You still need to pay the fee when you submit this application.
