Finalising your divorce

It’s usually quicker and easier if your husband or wife applies for the final order. This is because they are the applicant. You can save and sign out and ask them to apply, if it’s safe to do so.

If your husband or wife will not or cannot apply to finalise the divorce, then you can. You will need to apply for permission because you are the ‘respondent’ in the divorce process. You will also need to pay an application fee of £53, unless you are eligible for and apply for Help With Fees.

! Warning
If you have not finished negotiations about your money, property or other assets then you should seek legal advice before applying to finalise your divorce

If permission to apply for a final order is granted, then your application for a final order will also be considered.
