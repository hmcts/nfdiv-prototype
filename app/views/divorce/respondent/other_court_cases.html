Other court cases

The court needs to know if there are any other legal proceedings related to your marriage. This includes any legal proceedings that are:

  • ongoing, finished or abandoned
  • between you and your husband or wife
  • between you, your husband or wife and anyone else

Are there, or have there ever been, any other legal proceedings relating to your marriage?

Not including legal proceedings that may happen in the future.
