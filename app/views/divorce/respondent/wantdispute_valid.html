Disputing the divorce application

Your answers indicate that you want to dispute the divorce application. This is because:

  • you do not think your marriage is valid

If you want to dispute the divorce application then you’ll have to submit another form (known as ‘the answer’) with your reason for disputing. This will cost you £245, unless you are eligible for Help With Fees.

You will have until [21 days from the expiry of the 14 days] to submit the form. If you do not submit the form by the deadline, then your husband or wife will usually be able to continue with the divorce.
