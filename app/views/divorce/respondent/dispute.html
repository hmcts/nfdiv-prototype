Are you sure you want to dispute the divorce application

If you dispute the application for divorce then you’ll have to make another application with your reason for defending the divorce. This will cost you £245.

The only valid reasons for disputing the divorce are because (one or more of the following):

  • you do not think the courts of England and Wales have the legal power (jurisdiction) to grant the application
  • you want to make your own application for divorce
  • you do not think your marriage is valid
