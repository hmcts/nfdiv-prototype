Disputing the divorce application

If you want to dispute the application for divorce then you’ll have to submit another form (known as ‘the answer’) with your reason for disputing. This will cost you £245, unless you are eligible for Help With Fees.

Find out more about Help With Fees

You may be able to get help paying the fee if you (one or more of the following):

  • are on certain benefits
  • have a little or no savings
  • have low income

You will have until 29 October 2021 to submit the form. If you do not submit the form by the deadline, then your husband or wife will usually be able to continue with the divorce.

The only valid reasons for disputing the divorce are because (one or both of the following):

  • you do not think the courts of England and Wales have the legal power (jurisdiction) to grant the application
  • you do not believe your marriage is legally valid. For example, if one of you was already married or in a civil partnership when you got married
  • this marriage has already been legally ended
