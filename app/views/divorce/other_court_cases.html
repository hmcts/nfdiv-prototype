Other court cases relating your marriage, property or children

The court needs to know if there are any other legal proceedings related to your marriage, property or children. This includes any legal proceedings that are:

  • ongoing, finished or abandoned
  • between you and your {husband / wife}
  • between you, your {husband / wife} and anyone else

Are there, or have there ever been, any other legal proceedings relating to your marriage, property or children?

Do not include legal proceedings that may happen in the future.
