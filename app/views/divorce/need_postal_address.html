You need to get their address

You need an address for your husband or wife before you can apply for a divorce.

The address can be their postal address or their solicitor’s address. It can be UK or international. It cannot be their work address.

To find their address, you could try contacting their:

  • relatives
  • friends
  • last-known employer
  • trade union or professional organisation
