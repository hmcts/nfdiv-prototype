Applying for a financial order

You need to complete another form (Form A) and pay an additional fee of £255 to formally start financial proceedings. You will be given a link to Form A and more guidance after you have submitted this divorce application. You can get legal advice or ask a solicitor to draft a financial order for you.

The court will not start processing the financial order application until you have submitted Form A and paid the fee.
