You need to get a ‘certified translation’ of your marriage certificate

You need to get your marriage certificate translated into English and certified. You can find translation companies online which also do certification as part of their service.

When you have your translated and certified marriage certificate then return to this application and continue. You will be asked to upload a photo of it later, or post it in.

If you have a translated certificate that is not certified then you need to get it certified by a ‘notary public’. You can find a notary public through the Notaries Society or the Society of Scrivener Notaries.
