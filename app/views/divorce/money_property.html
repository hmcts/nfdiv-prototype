Dividing your money and property

It’s usually more straightforward and less expensive if you agree with your husband or wife on how to divide your money and property. There are mediation services available to help you come to an agreement. You will be given links to further guidance after you have submitted your application.

If you do agree then you can make the agreement legally binding. This is known as asking the court to make a ‘consent order’. You apply for a consent order separately by filling out a ‘D81’ paper application form.

If you disagree then you can ask the court to decide for you. This is known as applying for a ‘financial order’. The court needs to know now if you are planning on applying for a financial order.
