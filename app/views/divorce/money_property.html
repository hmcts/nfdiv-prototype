Dividing your money and property

If you agree with your husband about how to split your money, property, pensions and other assets, you can make your agreement legally binding. This is known as asking the court to make a ‘consent order’. You apply for a consent order separately by filling out a ‘D81’ paper application form

If you disagree with your husband about money and property then you can ask the court to decide for you. This is known as applying for a ‘financial order’. The court needs to know now if you are planning on applying for a financial order.

The court does not need to know if you are planning on applying for a consent order.
