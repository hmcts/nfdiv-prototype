Has your husband or wife given you a specific address where they want the divorce papers to be ‘served’ (sent)?

Your husband or wife may have given you an email or postal address where they want the divorce papers to be sent. For example, their solicitor’s address or an address which is not their home address.

If they have not, then you will be asked to provide their personal email and postal address, where the papers will be served (sent).
