Your names on your marriage certificate

The court needs to know the exact names that appear on your marriage certificate. These are the names you both used before you were married, such as maiden names.

! Warning Copy the marriage certificate exactly. For example, if it says ‘Sarah Brown (formerly known as Sarah Smith)’, then enter that.
