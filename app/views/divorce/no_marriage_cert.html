You need your marriage certificate

You need your marriage certificate to use this service.

You should ask your husband or wife for it, if they have it. Or you can order a certified copy online (if you got married in England or Wales).

If your original marriage certificate is not in English, it must be certified by the translator. You then need to get it certified by a ‘notary public’.
