What you need to do

The court is not yet satisfied you are entitled to get divorced. You need to update your application, before you can continue.

The court’s feedback

"The images of both the marriage certificate and the Respondents deed poll are too small, so that when they are enlarged, they become blurred and cannot be read and checked. Please provide new images of the marriage certificate and Respondent’s deed poll.

You have said your life is mainly based in England and Wales but I note the respondent says you live in Scotland. If this is correct then the courts of England or Wales may not have jurisdiction. We must be satisfied you’re your life is mainly based in England or Wales to prove the courts have jurisdiction over your divorce. Please provide specific details of your life, including where you live and work."

You can download a copy of the court’s full Refusal Order (PDF).

What you need to do next

You will need to change the application, and submit it again. You will also need to pay a £95 amendment fee.

The application will then need to be sent to your husband or wife again so that they can respond.

You will receive an email when the conditional order has been granted by the court.
