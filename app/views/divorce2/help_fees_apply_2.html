You need to apply for help with your divorce fees

You need to apply for help with your fees (opens in a new tab) before you continue with this divorce application. You will then receive a ‘Help with fees’ reference number. You can enter this when you return to this application.

You will be asked to enter a ‘court or tribunal form number’ when you apply for help with fees. You should enter ‘D8’ when asked. This is the divorce application form number.
