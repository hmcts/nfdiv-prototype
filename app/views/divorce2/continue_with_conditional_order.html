Do you want to continue with your divorce?

The next step in the divorce process is to apply for a ‘conditional order’. This shows the court agrees that you can get a divorce.

Read more about the next steps

You have to complete 2 more steps before you are legally divorced:

  1. Apply for a conditional order.

    2. This shows that the court agrees that you’re entitled to get a divorce.

  2. Apply for a final order.

    3. This legally ends the marriage. You cannot apply for a final order until 6 weeks after the conditional order.
