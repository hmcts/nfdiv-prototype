If you want to settle your finances before finalising your divorce

Save and sign out and settle your finances before applying for a final order. You can apply any time until . If you apply after that then you will need to explain the delay to the court.

If you do not apply then your husband or wife can apply for a final order from . If they apply then you will both need to come to a court hearing.

If you do not want to get divorced anymore

If you and your husband or wife do want to get divorced anymore then you can withdraw your application. Phone or email the HMCTS contact centre using the details on this page or on any correspondence you’ve received. Make sure you include your case number: EZ111456
