Latest update

You have responded to the divorce application. You do not have to do anything further.

The next step is for your husband or wife to apply for a 'conditional order'. This is a document that says that the court does not see any reason why you cannot divorce.

Your husband or wife can apply for a conditional order from 9 February 2022. This is because they have to wait 20 weeks from when the divorce application was issued. You will receive an email when the conditional order has been granted.

After the conditional order, they need to apply for a final order, which legally ends the marriage. This cannot be made until 6 weeks after the conditional order

You can use the time to decide how your money and property will be divided. This is dealt with separately to the divorce application. Find out more about dividing money and property
