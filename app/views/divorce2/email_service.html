How to apply to serve (deliver) the papers another way

You have to make a separate application to serve the divorce papers another way. You will be given a link to the form after you have submitted this application. It will be reviewed by a judge and costs an additional £50.

For example, you could apply to have the papers served (sent) by email, text message or social media. For example, you could apply to have the papers served (delivered) by the email address you entered earlier.
