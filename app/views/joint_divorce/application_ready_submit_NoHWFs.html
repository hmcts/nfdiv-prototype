Pay and submit

Your joint application has been agreed by you and your husband or wife. You need to pay the application fee of £550 before it can be submitted. The payment system does not allow you to split the payment.

You cannot use help with fees on this application because your husband or wife did not apply for help with fees. Both of you need to apply and be eligible in a joint application.

If you cannot pay

The payment system will only allow you to pay, but you could talk to your husband or wife about whether they would be prepared to send you some money.

Or you could submit a sole application. If you apply as a sole applicant, only you have to apply for Help With Fees.

If you create a sole application, you will not have to re-enter the information you have already provided. You will need to provide some new information though.

You can submit a sole application here.

This joint application will not be submitted until you pay the fee.
