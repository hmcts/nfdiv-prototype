You have not been married long enough to apply for a divorce

You can only apply for divorce after you have been married for at least one year, in England or Wales.

You can wait until it has been a year since you got married and then apply. Or if you cannot wait then you can apply for a legal separation.

If you believe your marriage was never legally valid or is ‘voidable’, then you may be able to annul your marriage.
