Withdrawing your divorce application

You have said you do not want to continue with your divorce. If you want to withdraw your application then you and your husband or wife need to fill out a separate D11 form and send it to the court. Details of where to send it are on the form.

If you want to continue as a sole applicant
If you still want to get divorced but you do not think your husband or wife will confirm the next stage of this joint application, then you can change to a sole application. This means your husband or wife will become the respondent for the rest of the divorce.

You will be able to change to a sole application after you have confirmed your application for a conditional order. If your husband or wife does not also confirm the application within 2 weeks then you will receive an email with details of how to change your application online. You should continue to apply for a conditional order.

If you want to change your application to a sole application now then you need to download and fill out a D84 paper form. Details of where to post it are on the form. If you want to choose this option then you should save and sign out.
