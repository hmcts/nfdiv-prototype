Your marriage certificate

You’ll be asked to enter some information from the marriage certificate, and upload a digital photo of it later in this application.

It must be a photo of the original marriage certificate or a certified copy. You can order a certified copy online (opens in a new tab), if you got married in England or Wales.

If the original certificate is not in English, you’ll need to provide a certified translation.
