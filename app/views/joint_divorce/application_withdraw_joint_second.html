Withdrawing your divorce application

You have said you do not want to continue with your divorce. If you want to withdraw your application then you and your husband or wife need to fill out a separate D11 form and send it to the court. Details of where to send it are on the form.

If you want to continue as a sole applicant
Your husband or wife has already confirmed your joint application for divorce. The quickest way for you to progress your application is for you to continue and confirm your joint application.

If you want to change to a sole application then it will delay the divorce application. You will have to make a seperate application by downloading and filling out a D84 paper form. Details of where to send it are on the form. You should save and sign out and send in the form if you want to change to a sole application.

The quickest way to progress your divorce application is to confirm your joint application.
