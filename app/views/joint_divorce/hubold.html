Dividing your money and property

It’s usually more straightforward and less expensive if you agree with your husband or wife on how to divide your money and property. Get help agreeing.

If you do agree then you can make the agreement legally binding. This is known as asking the court to make a ‘consent order’.

If you disagree then you can ask the court to decide for you. This is known as applying for a ‘financial order’.

Read the guidance on money and property when you divorce or separate.
