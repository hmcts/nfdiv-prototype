Changing to a sole application

The quickest way to progress your application is for your husband or wife to apply for a conditional order. They have been emailed details of how to do this. You should contact them to remind them, if it’s safe to do so.

If you do not think they will apply then you can change to a sole application. This means you will become the main applicant and they will become the ‘respondent’ for the rest of divorce process. They will be notified of this change.

! Warning
You will not be able to change back to a joint application once you have made this change.
