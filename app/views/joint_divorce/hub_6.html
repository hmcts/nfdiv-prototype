Latest update

You and your husband or wife have applied for a conditional order.

The court will check your application and send it to a judge. If the judge agrees that there is no reason why you should not get a divorce, then they will ‘pronounce’ your conditional order in court. You will receive an email by after your application has been checked. This will have the time, date and court where your conditional order will be pronounced.

After your conditional order is pronounced, you then have to apply for a ‘final order’. This will finalise your divorce. You have to wait 6 weeks until after your conditional order, to apply for the final order.
