How do you want to apply for the divorce?

You can apply for the divorce on your own (as a ‘sole applicant’) or with your {{ data['husband-wife'] }} (in a ‘joint application’).

Applying as a sole applicant

If you apply as a sole applicant, your {{ data['husband-wife'] }} responds to your divorce application after you have submitted it. You will be applying on your own.

Applying jointly, with your {{ data['husband-wife'] }}

If you apply jointly, your {{ data['husband-wife'] }} joins and reviews this online application before it’s submitted. You will be applying together.

How you divide your money and property is dealt with separately. It should not affect your decision on whether to do a sole or a joint application.

You have applied for Help With Fees. If you choose to do a joint application, then your {{ data['husband-wife'] }} must also apply and be eligible for Help With Fees. If they are not eligible or do not apply, then you will be asked to pay the full application fee. In a sole application, only you needed to have applied.

You should have already discussed this with your {{ data['husband-wife'] }}
