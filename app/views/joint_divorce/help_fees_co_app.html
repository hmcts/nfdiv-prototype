Help with the divorce fee

This divorce application costs £550. This service will not ask you to pay the fee. Your husband or wife will be asked to pay because they are the first applicant.

Your husband or wife has said they need help paying the fee. They can only use Help With Fees on this application if you claim and are eligible for Help With Fees too.

You can claim Help With Fees if you: (one or more of the following):

  • are on certain benefits
  • have a little or no savings
  • have low income

Do you need help with fees?

Your husband or wife can only use help with fees, if you apply and are eligible for Help With Fees too. You will not be asked to pay the fee by this service, no matter which answer you select.
