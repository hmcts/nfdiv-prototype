You cannot apply to get a divorce

Your marriage must have irretrievably broken down for you to get a divorce. This is the law in England and Wales.

If you end this joint application then your husband or wife will be notified by email. If they still want to get a divorce then they can apply as a sole applicant. This would mean you would have to respond to divorce application. You can also launch a sole application, if you want.

If you are not sure what to do then you can save and sign out and get legal advice.
