How to finalise your divorce

The quickest way to finalise your divorce is to ask your husband or wife to confirm your joint application for a final order. They have been emailed details of how to do this.

If they will not confirm then you can apply for a final order as a sole applicant. First your husband or wife needs to be given 2 week’s notice that you are intending to apply. This is so they are aware that the marriage is about to be legally ended.
